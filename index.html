Hi!

Sandipani School, Nagpur is a coeducational English medium day school affiliated with the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE), New Delhi. It offers schooling from nursery through class XII. Sandipani aims to create a child centred school where the child is the nucleus and the teacher and the parent are on the periphery, by taking the psychology of the child into consideration and designing the syllabus around it.

"Sandipani is a distinctive, leading Indian school known for its intellectual rigor, world-class teachers, campus of stunning beauty and an atmosphere that renders learning fun. Through its curriculum, the engagement of the faculty, and life on the campus, challenges students to be enlightened thinkers and prepares them to be global citizens who will thrive in life and in work. Learning here, living here, on entering the campus, you feel it: We are always bustling with never-ending energy and creativity. Sandipani is a school that has set new benchmarks in the field of education & continues to do so. Visit our campus to experience the Sandipani Life. #sandipanilife"

Campus Life


The School Campus
The school has one of the best infrastructure set up to let our young buds flourish in all respects. A vast expanse of 8 acres provides a perfect ambience for an aesthetically chartered school building.

E-learning
All the classrooms across the school are enabled with smart boards facilitating the learning of students. Subject wise e-content is available for teachers and students.

Library
The spacious and fully equipped libraries serve as storehouse of knowledge and information for our students of all age groups. Books and literature on all subjects are available for reference.

Laboratories
The school has fully equipped science, mathematics, exploratory and language labs with upgraded equipments. Computer labs with computers of latest configuration complement the teaching learning process.

Garden Library
An attractive environment in the heart of nature where students feel free despite being in the formal learning environment of the school.
